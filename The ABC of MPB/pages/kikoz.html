Who is Kiko Zambianchi

Kiko Zambianchi (Francisco José Zambianchi) born in Ribeirão Preto, São Paulo state. He is a Brazilian composer and singer.
He started playing guitar during his teenage years, having participated in student festivals and theater plays, did shows in the interior, formed the band Vida de Rua and, at age 23, moved to São Paulo where he was hired in a month by EMI .
He released, at the end of 1984, the single of "Rolam as Pedras", that burst in the radios of São Paulo and later in all Brazil. After that, Zambianchi launches, by EMI, the LP Shock, which has recorded three hits: the title track, "Rolam as Pedras". With participation of Lulu Santos and Marina Lima in the songs "Nossa Energia" and "Quem sofre sou eu", respectively. "No meio da rua", another track of the album, enters the soundtrack of the program Armação Limitada of Rede Globo. Marina Lima records "I love you" by Kiko and bursts into the radio stations all over the country, and in the same year, Erasmo Carlos records another song composed by Zambianchi called "Manchas e Intrigas". "Primeiros Erros" was a song practically discarded by the label, which, after the success of "Rolam as Pedras" and "Shock", wanted Kiko already to leave for the recording of a new album. But Kiko, believing in the success of the song, made personal divulgation of the song, going to the radio stations of the great São Paulo with the disc to be played, and gradually the music began to be known and performed, and reached the top of the charts in all Brazil.
