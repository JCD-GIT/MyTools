Who is Belchior

During his childhood, in Ceará, he was a fair-haired singer and poet. He studied choral music and piano with Acácio Halley. His father played flute and saxophone and his mother sang in church choir. There were poets and bohemians. As a child, she was influenced by radio singers Ângela Maria, Cauby Peixoto and Nora Ney. He was a radio programmer in Sobral. In 1962, he moved to Fortaleza, where he studied Philosophy and Humanities. He began studying medicine, but left the course in the fourth year, in 1971, to dedicate himself to the artistic career. He joined a group of young composers and musicians, such as Fagner, Ednardo, Rodger Rogério, Teti, Cirino among others, known as the Personnel of Ceará [1].
