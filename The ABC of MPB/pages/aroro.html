Who is Angela Roro

Angela Maria Diniz Gonçalves (born in Rio de Janeiro on December 5, 1949), best known by the stage name Angela Ro Ro, based on an onomatopoeia of her laughter,[1] is a Brazilian singer-songwriter influenced by her idols Ella Fitzgerald, Maysa Matarazzo and Jacques Brel.[2] Her debut album was voted by Rolling Stone one of the greatest Brazilian albums of all time.[3] She is one of the first out lesbian singers in Brazil.
